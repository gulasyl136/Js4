FRANCE

France is a state in Western Europe. Political system - republic.

CAPITAL AND LARGEST CITIES

The capital is Paris. Large cities: Paris, Rouen, Bordeaux, Toulouse, Marseille, Nice, Strasbourg, Lyon, Lille, Nantes, Lyon.

LANGUAGE

The official language is French. German is spoken in some areas, and local dialects are also spoken

National dishes of French cuisine.

CROISSANT. It was from France that this came to us. For the French, coffee with a croissant is a traditional breakfast.

Frog legs. Not everyone decides to try this original French delicacy. Frog legs are very tasty and resemble chicken.

Snails in garlic sauce. Another unique recipe from French cuisine, which is definitely worth trying.

What is France famous for all over the world?

France occupies a leading place in Europe in the production of wheat, milk, meat, potatoes, sugar beets and grapes. The country has a tradition in clothing production and the fashion industry.

French wines, furniture, jewelry, perfumes and cosmetics are famous all over the world.

EDUCATION

Higher education in France is recognized as one of the best not only in Europe, but in the world. According to UNESCO, France ranks fourth after the USA, Great Britain and Australia

WHAT IS UNIQUE ABOUT FRANCE ?

The unique of this country are morning coffee with a croissant and cozy tables in the Latin Quarter of Paris, as well as the famous Eiffel Tower. The country is also famous with French baguette and Camembert cheese.
